AntiPesto Readme File¶

Antipesto is a DIY IoT project for a backyard trapper to wirelessly monitor their own pest traps over a short distance. In a homage to the casual inventor, the project title is inspired by the name of Wallace and Grommit’s pest control business “Anti-Pesto” from the 2005 film Wallace & Gromit: The Curse of the Were-Rabbit”
